2. Photo-Police

Most malicious photo manipulations are created using standard image editing tools, such as
Adobe Photoshop.We present a method for detecting one very popular Photoshop manipulation
image warping applied to human faces using a model trained entirely using fake images that were
automatically generated by scripting Photoshop itself. We show that our model outperforms humans
at the task of ecognizing manipulated images, can predict the specific location of edits, and in some
casescan be used to “undo” a manipulation to reconstruct the original, unedited image. We demonstrate
that the system can be successfully applied to real, artist-created image manipulations
